How It Works

Simple steps to start winning big prizes in our blockchain lottery

1
🔗

Connect Your Wallet

Connect your MetaMask wallet to the BSC Testnet. Make sure you have some BNB for gas fees and USDT for ticket purchases. It's quick and secure!

2
✅

Approve USDT

Approve USDT spending for the lottery contract. This is a one-time approval that allows you to purchase multiple tickets without repeated approvals.

3
🎫

Buy Tickets

Choose your round and select how many tickets to buy (1-10 per transaction). Each ticket gives you a chance to win amazing prizes in the draw.

4
🎰

Wait for Draw

Once all tickets are sold, the smart contract automatically conducts a fair and transparent draw to select winners. Everything is verifiable on-chain.

5
🏆

Claim Prizes

If you win, claim your prize directly from the smart contract. Winners are automatically eligible for instant payouts - no waiting, no hassle.
